About Cash Castle

Most Americans struggle to stick to a budget, to stay out of consumer debt, and to save up an emergency fund - let alone save for retirement!

But you're different.

You're here because you want to control your money, not let it control you. Cash Castle was designed to be a fun and interactive sandbox for playing with your personal finances. You can tinker with the numbers until they're perfect, visualize your pathways out of debt, and calculate how much compound interest can supercharge your investments over time. Jump in and have fun building your own Cash Castle!

We would love to hear from you. Please contact us if you have questions, suggestions, or just want to chat!
